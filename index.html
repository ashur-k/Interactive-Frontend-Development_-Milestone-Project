    Covid-19 Symptoms

    National Health Service: High temperature you can feel hot on your back and chest. A new contious cough, this means couging more than an hour or or 3 or more coughing episodes in 24 hours.

    More Information

    Centers for Disease Control and Prevention: Cough, shortness of breath or difficulty in breathing, fever, chills, repeated shaking with chills, headache, muscle pain, sore throat, new loss of taste or smell.

    More Information

    World Health Organisation: Common symptoms include, fever, tiredness, dry cough. Ohter symptoms include, shortness of breath, aches and pains, sore throath and in some cases diarrhoea, nausea or a runny nose.
